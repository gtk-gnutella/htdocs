NO Software Patents in Europe

10 days of netstrike or 20 years of software patents

We are protesting against software patents in Europe.

This website and software may become illegal to use in Europe if this dangerous directive is adopted without proper amending.

The Commission and the Council of Ministers are pushing for unlimited patentability of software, heavily lobbied by multinationals and patent lawyers. They are ignoring the democratically voted decision of the European Parliament from 24 september 2003, which has the support of more than 300.000 citizens, 2.000.000 SMEs and dozens of economists and scientists.

Please join us in a demonstration in the streets of Brussels on 14 april 2004.

You can also join this online protest by closing your homepage from 9 until 15 may, and 9 until 13 june.
